The best movies according to Xipra.

My top three movies of all the time.

Yeh Jawaani Hai Deewani

This movie revolves around the life of a girl named Naina.
She flees away from her home to go to a trip to Manali which is a mesmorising mountainous place situateed in northern part of Himachal Pradesh.
She is portrayed as an introvert person and watching her fall in love with the school's playboy seems interesting.

Zindagi Na Milegi Dobara

Three friends commit to have a bachelorate party with three adventure sports of their choice.
However, which adventure sport is chosen by each one has to be hidden till performing.
Their friendship is depicted beautifully and the way they overcome their fears is marvelous.

Dear Zindagi

Being left behind with her grandparents, Naina is a girl who is extremely ambitious.
Her parents always favoured and took care of her younger brother ignoring her due to which she has to go to therapy.
There she falls for her therapist.
